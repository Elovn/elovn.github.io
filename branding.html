Pulso Vital Consulting Group

Pulso Vital Consulting Group is a HR consulting firm established in Mexico City on which I worked on the redesign of its graphic identity. The project consisted in the development of a new branding concept that showed the innovative approach of the company. The logo was inspired in architecture photography, which shows geometric figures and a gradient of a combination of pastel colors.

the logo design

The logo is composed by an hexagon formed by four geometric figures, which represent the organization of the company and the importance of teamwork. It is a form that can be deconstructed to create a new one, implying creativity and innovation. The second logo presents some color modifications suggested by the client, showing the pulse that the company offers through its services.
