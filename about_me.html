I'M a
DESIGNER

I am a fashion designer based in Mexico City, I love to develop research projects in design, sustainability and technology. I believe in experimentation, innovation and co-working as a way to create projects.

I studied Fashion Design and Clothing Production in CEDIM (Monterrey, Mexico). Where I started my sustainable fashion design thesis project, and developed innovative textiles with PET. Now I want to learn and use with experience working in favor social wellness and nature preservation.

If you are interested in collaborating on a project, I would like to hear about you!
