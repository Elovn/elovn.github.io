MAKUN

MAKUN starts on march of 2013 with the idea of create a fashion brand by producing innovative garments through pattern design and the application of embroidery items to avoid the trend of fast fashion. The main idea is to maintain the experimentation in the design and production process, working with asymmetric cuts and embroidery to create textures.

inspiration

The random forms of nature are our inspiration. Working with the floral textiles and asymmetric cuts create the special visual effect of the unexpected natural behaviour.

the process

Our process consist in experimenting to create visual effects on garments by using different cuts of geometric figures.
